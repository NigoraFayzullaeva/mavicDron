What is Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel ideal for aerial photography. The drone has all the best technology DJI, it will transform the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro features the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. The L1D-20c camera works according to a unique Hasselblad Natural Color Solution (HNCS) 5 technology, allowing users to take great pictures from the air with a resolution of 20 megapixels and stunning colors.

What are the differences between the Mavic 2 Pro and the Mavic 2 Zoom?

The Mavic 2 has improved almost every aspect: camera, video transmission, flight time, speed, level noise detection, multi-directional obstacle detection, intelligent functions and unique function Hyperlapse (hyperlapse).

Why is the Mavic 2 better than the Mavic Pro?

The Mavic 2's low-noise propellers and newly designed chassis make it to DJI's most advanced aircraft. That means 19% less body drag than the Mavic Pro. The results are: Longer flight time lifted up from 27 min to 31 min due to higher battery capacity lower power consumption.

Can the Mavic 2 be connected to DJI Goggles?

0500 or above on your computer using the appropriate version of DJI Assistant 2. Power on the DJI Goggles, choose Linking in Settings, then choose Mavic 2. Press the link button on the Mavic 2 and wait for a few seconds to connect DJI Goggles to the aircraft.

Is the Mavic 2 waterproof？

The Mavic 2 Pro and Zoom are not waterproof. There are waterproof drones on the market but the DJI Mavic 2 editions is not waterproof.
